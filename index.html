Work

Selected projects

Key projects where I drove impact through organization, automation, and collaboration.

Case 01

Office Workflow Automation

Designed and implemented an automated document routing system that reduced processing time by 40% and eliminated manual data entry errors across departments.

The system replaced a manual, email-based approval chain with a rules-based router that assigned documents to the right reviewer automatically, flagged missing fields before submission, and logged every step for audit purposes. Rollout included training three teams and writing the supporting SOP.

Case 02

Centralized Documentation System

Built a unified knowledge base consolidating SOPs, reports, and compliance documents into a searchable, version-controlled platform accessible to all team members.

Previously, documentation was scattered across shared drives and inboxes with no version history. The new structure introduced consistent naming conventions, folder taxonomy, and access permissions, cutting the average time to find a document from minutes to seconds and eliminating duplicate, outdated copies in circulation.

Case 03

AI-Powered Reporting Tool

Leveraged AI tools to automate weekly report generation, cutting preparation time from 6 hours to 30 minutes while improving data accuracy and visualization quality.

Raw data exports were previously compiled and formatted by hand each week. An AI-assisted pipeline now cleans the data, generates summary visuals, and drafts the narrative commentary for review, leaving the team more time for analysis instead of formatting.
